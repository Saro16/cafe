HISTORY

Mimi's Cafe was founded in 1978 by Arthur J. Simms in Anaheim, California. Simms named the restaurant after his French grandmother, Mimi, and sought to create a dining experience that reflected her warm hospitality and love for French cuisine.From its inception, Mimi's Cafe distinguished itself by offering a menu inspired by French cooking traditions. Dishes such as quiche, croissants, French onion soup, and crepes were among the restaurant's signature offerings.
